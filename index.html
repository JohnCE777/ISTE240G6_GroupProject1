C.R.A.P - Creating Better Websites

Crap is an slang word for excrement. It is also an acronym that stands for:

  • Contrast
  • Repetition
  • Allignment
  • Proximity
These design priciples serve as a guide to make websites that are both easier to navigate and visually appealing. Robin Patricia Williams introduced CRAP in her book, The Non-Designer's Design Book which was published in 2011. Since the book's publication, the CRAP principles have become a standard on the web.
